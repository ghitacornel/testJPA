in MANY to MANY relationship both entities are independent
in MANY to MANY relationship both entities must know of the relationship
MANY to MANY relationship is a form of ONE TO MANY + MANY TO ONE relationship working in both ways

in MANY to MANY relationship both entities are independent => CASCADING of any type is forbidden

in MANY to MANY relationship only one entity owns the relationship.
due to relationship owning concept in JPA when removing or adding a relationship between two entities
the relationship must be removed or added on both sides.
